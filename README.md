# About Me:
I am an aspiring VLSI and Semiconductor Engineer with strong fundamentals in Digital & Analog Electronics, CMOS, STA, RTL Design, FPGA Prototyping, and both Full-Custom and Semi-Custom VLSI Design methodologies. My academic and hands-on project experience has strengthened my understanding of CMOS design flows, ASIC/FPGA Design, and industry-standard EDA tools. I am passionate about chip design, verification, Frontend & Backend roles and semiconductor technology development. I enjoy solving complex hardware problems and continuously learning about advancements in the VLSI domain. Currently, I am seeking internship or entry-level opportunities in Digital/Analog IC Design, RTL Design & Verification, Physical Design, FPGA-based System Development. I am eager to contribute to impactful semiconductor innovations while expanding my technical skills and industry exposure.
